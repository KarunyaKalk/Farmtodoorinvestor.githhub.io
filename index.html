Our Core Value Proposition

Farm2Door addresses key pain points in the agricultural supply chain by leveraging technology to create a fair and transparent marketplace.

Eliminate Middlemen

Direct digital marketplace connecting farmers directly with buyers, removing multiple intermediaries that reduce farmer profits.

Fair Pricing

Farmers set their prices while buyers see transparent comparisons with mandi prices, ensuring fair compensation and savings.

Trust & Transparency

Verified sellers, quality ratings, clear pricing, and farmer stories create a trustworthy ecosystem for all participants.

Simple User Journey

From crop listing to delivery and feedback, Farm2Door provides a seamless experience for both farmers and buyers.

1

Farmer Lists Crop

Uploads crop info with price & photo

2

Buyer Searches

Searches for specific crops & location

3

Price Comparison

4

Order & Payment

Places order & pays directly to farmer

5

Delivery

Farmer arranges delivery with tracking

6

Feedback

Buyer rates farmer & leaves feedback

Example Transaction

A buyer searches for "Wheat, Telangana" → sees verified farmers → selects farmer → sees: market price ₹28/kg vs farmer price ₹24/kg → places order → pays ₹24/kg directly to farmer → farmer arranges delivery → buyer receives order → rates farmer.
